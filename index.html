Our Innovation

AI-Powered Patient Monitoring

CareBotix combines computer vision, environmental sensing, and intelligent alerts for comprehensive patient care.

How It Works

  • Real-time Pose Detection: YOLOv8-based monitoring for fall detection and posture analysis

  • Environmental Monitoring: Temperature, humidity, sound, and distance tracking

  • Smart Alerts: Immediate notifications for falls, abnormal postures, or environmental concerns

  • AI Assistant: Gemini-powered chatbot for instant status updates and medical insights

Technical Advantages

Hardware Components

HD Camera, Temperature sensor (±0.5°C), Humidity sensor (±2%), Sound level meter (30-130dB), Ultrasonic distance sensor (2-400cm)

Software Stack

YOLOv8, Google Gemini Pro, MongoDB, PyQt6, REST APIs, WebSocket

Performance

95% accuracy in fall detection, <100ms latency, supports 10 concurrent video streams
